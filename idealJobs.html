Jacopo's - Ideal Job

Ideal Job In IT

My Ideal job in IT would be Frontend Web Developer/Designer.

My goal is to work as a web developer and designer. This would allow me to use both my creative background and what I will learn during this course of study.

A front-end developer/designer is required to both convert data to graphical interfaces (Web development) and create visual designs, animations and graphics (Web design). Programming skills, as well as a good understanding of the social media environment, are required for this position. Moreover, creativity and good communication skills seem to be extremely relevant to this role. Successful candidates would ideally have an IT background, with programming experience, paired with the ability to create graphic designs and concepts with Adobe CC.

It is very important for me, after a career in the arts, to be able to work in a position that can express my creative skills while applying new programming skills necrssary in the IT world.

Hamilton's - Ideal Job

Ideal Job In IT

My ideal job is something along the lines of a Cyber Security Analyst.

For now, my goal is to be working in the Cybersecurity filed in the future which will require further study that I am willing to undertake. I have chosen this role not only out of interest but out of the fact the job is in demand.

A Cyber security analyst has the role of monitoring and keeping secure a company's or organisations network and services. There main roles are to monitor security, prevent breaches using various software and tactics and to act quickly to resolve any breaches or data theft that may occur. The demand for Cyber security analysts is growing just as fast as our innovation and development of new technology is, because of this Cyber security analysts need to be innovative and good problem solvers, the ever changing landscape of I.T is constantly providing new problems and challenges that must be addressed quickly and creatively.

Although I wont learn everything I will require to fill this role studying the current course I am, I believe it will at least provide a path that will lead to further study and set a solid foundation for myself to build upon.
